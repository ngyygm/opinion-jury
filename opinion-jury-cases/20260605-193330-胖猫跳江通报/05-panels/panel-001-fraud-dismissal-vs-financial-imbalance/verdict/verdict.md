## Deliberation Summary

This verdict is based solely on the public transcript (36 turns across 6 rounds from 6 anonymous speakers) and the CLAIM_GATE fact verification packet (48 verified claims). The adjudicator was blind to all actor identities, role cards, private thoughts, and filing metadata.

### Evolution of the Discussion

The debate began with a clear split in Round 1:
- **4 speakers** (SEAT-01, 02, 03, 06) accepted the police conclusion
- **1 speaker** (SEAT-04) strongly rejected it based on emotional intuition
- **1 speaker** (SEAT-05) maintained strategic neutrality with selective framing

By Round 6, all six speakers had converged toward accepting the legal framework, with the primary opponent (SEAT-04) completing a dramatic transformation from "definitely fraud" to "the real question is why no one helped him."

### Where Content Resists Distortion

The following facts proved remarkably resistant to motivated reinterpretation across all six rounds:

1. **Bidirectional transfers**: 317 outgoing (799K) vs. 179 incoming (463K) -- cited by every speaker
2. **Shared savings account** with Pangmao withdrawing 75K more than he deposited (including 71K of Tan's deposits)
3. **Family introduction**: Tan brought Pangmao home to meet parents during Spring Festival 2024
4. **Voluntary annotation**: Final transfer of 66,666.66 yuan explicitly annotated "voluntary gift" by Pangmao
5. **Procedural closure**: Sister accepted non-filing decision, families reconciled, full economic difference returned
6. **Investigation depth**: 6-day investigation with complete transfer and chat record review

### Real-World Risk Assessment

**Risk Level: MODERATE-LOW**

The verified facts are resilient to distortion. The primary vulnerability is the emotional gap between legal conclusions and public intuition -- a gap that is inherent to the case rather than a communication failure. The discussion demonstrated that even motivated reinterpretation converges toward constructive social questions (mental health support, crisis signal recognition) rather than sustained personal attacks.
